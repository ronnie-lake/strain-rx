About Ocimene

Ocimene is recognized by its sweet, fragrant, herbaceous, and woodsy aromas which help plants defend themselves in their natural environment. Ocimene occurs naturally in botanicals as diverse as mint, parsley, pepper, basil, mangoes, orchids, kumquats, and of course cannabis. Ocimene has a low boiling point, so to get the most out of this terpene, using a vaporizer on a low temperature setting.

Ocimene’s Effects & Medical Benefits

Ocimene is a decongestant with antiseptic properties. It has anti-viral, anti-fungal, and anti-bacterial benefits and multiple studies have shown it to be effective against inflammation.
