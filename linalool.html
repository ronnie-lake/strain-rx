About Linalool

Linalool is a terpene frequently used in aromatherapy because of its fragrant lavender aroma. It smells like spring flowers with undertones of spice.

Linalool’s Effects & Medical Benefits

Known to induce relaxation, Linalool is a terpene that’s favored by medical cannabis users who are want to overcome opioid addiction. It is very soothing and quickly helps to overcome anxiety, reduces pain, and stops convulsions. Linalool also increased social behavior and decreased aggressiveness. In oil form, it has been reported to help with burns and acne. Due to it’s sedative effects, Linalool can also be implemented as a sleep aid.
